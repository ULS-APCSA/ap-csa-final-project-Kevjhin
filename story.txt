GAME NAME: DEAD CITY
INTRO:
  In the year 2074, an unknown virus breaks out, countless people die from this unknown virus, and countless people are trapped in the blocked city. 
  Twenty years later, the government prevents citizens from going out to find materials and restricts Internet communication, leading to the voice of resistance, 
  and finally one day, the war breaks out, and your city is also involved, please try to escape from this dead city.
